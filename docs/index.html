Real Estate Calculators

📝

ARV

(After Repair Value) is the estimated market value of a property after all repairs and renovations are completed.

💰

MAO

Maximum Allowable Offer: MAO = (ARV × 70%) - Repair Costs. This determines the highest price an investor should pay.

💵

CoC

Cash-on-Cash Return: CoC = (Annual Cash Flow / Cash Invested) × 100. Measures the return on actual cash invested.

📊

Cap Rate

Capitalization Rate: Cap Rate = (Net Operating Income / Property Value) × 100. Used to evaluate profitability.

🏢

GRM

Gross Rent Multiplier: GRM = Property Price / Gross Annual Rental Income. Helps compare rental properties.

📈

DSCR

Debt Service Coverage Ratio: DSCR = Net Operating Income / Annual Debt Payments. Used by lenders to assess coverage.

🏦

LTV

Loan-to-Value Ratio: LTV = (Loan Amount / Property Value) × 100. Measures lending risk; lower is better.

⚖️

BER

Break-Even Ratio: BER = (Operating Expenses + Debt Payments) / Gross Income × 100. Shows income needed for expenses.

📊

ROI

Return on Investment: ROI = (Net Profit / Total Investment) × 100. A general measure of profitability.

🏠

Vacancy Rate

Vacancy Rate = (Vacant Units / Total Units) × 100. Helps evaluate potential income loss in rental properties.
